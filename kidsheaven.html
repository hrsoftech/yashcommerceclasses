Welcome to

Kid’s Heaven Preschool

Welcome to Kid’s Heaven Preschool, a nurturing haven in the heart of Gorakhpur where early childhood blossoms into a lifelong journey of learning, exploration, and joy. At Kid’s Heaven, we believe that every child is unique, full of potential, and deserving of an environment that encourages curiosity, creativity, and confidence from the very beginning. Established with the vision of creating a strong foundation for lifelong learning, Kid’s Heaven Preschool has become a trusted name among parents seeking a safe, stimulating, and caring environment for their children. We take pride in being more than just a preschool – we are a second home where children laugh, play, learn, and grow.
